Meet Paul

Paul Graham Jr. writes to share what he is learning, what he is questioning, and what he believes is worth exploring more deeply. His work moves across personal experience, practical ideas, business thinking, sales, AI, mindset, energy, and the kinds of topics that stay with people after the conversation ends.

Some books are built around lived experience. Others are built around ideas, research, and thoughtful interpretation. Together, they reflect a body of work meant to inform, challenge, and leave people with something meaningful to think about.

He is also the Founder & President of Dream Chaser Nation.

Sell This to Your Mom

Sell This to Your Mom — The mother of all books. This starts the journey. Just as your own journey begins straight from the womb and your first love — Mom.

The one who takes care of you and nurtures you to be the best that you can set out to be. The first one to love you unconditionally and connect with you before you ever even enter this world.

This book is to remind all of us, myself included, to stay on the path of doing the right thing. Sometimes it is easy to slowly or quickly travel slightly off of that path. This compelling question of Would you Sell This To Your Mom? reminds us to stay on the right journey — to ensure our moral base in this tough competitive environment of sales stays on track — on the right path.

This book introduces us to a bunch of little tidbits of lightbulb moments and into little pockets that really influenced my sales career — the little ways of understanding things, and also big picture clarity.

This book sets the pathway for all of the rest of the books. Everything webs out from this book. The Sales Logic epiphany concept came to me while writing this book, and I couldn’t jot it all down fast enough. I wanted to capture those thoughts before they eluded me forever.

I changed how I sold, and how I wanted to think about the entire sales process — all through the lens of using logic as the base and ensuring the concept of Selling This To Your Mom should always permeate every organization and sales process.

Sales Logic

Sales Logic expands the foundation — diving deeper into formal logic and how it shapes clear, structured thinking.

This book explores logic at a broader level first, then applies that framework directly to sales.

It focuses on how different forms of reasoning work behind the scenes, helping you think more clearly, navigate decisions in real time, and operate with precision.

It also introduces the crossover between human decision-making and computer logic — showing how logic serves as the foundation for how machines operate.

This added layer creates a deeper perspective, giving readers exposure to concepts they may not have explored otherwise.

AI Mastermind

AI Mastermind is the natural next step for those drawn deeper into the world of artificial intelligence.

It builds directly from concepts introduced in Sales Logic, expanding that foundation into a more complete AI journey.

This book continues where that curiosity leads — taking ideas that couldn’t fully fit into one chapter and developing them into a full, structured path of understanding.

It brings together foundational AI knowledge with more advanced concepts, while keeping everything practical and accessible — even for those with little to no coding experience.

Designed for both sales professionals and those outside the space, it provides a complete entry point into AI — helping readers move from zero knowledge to a level of understanding beyond most.

Emotions and Feelings

Emotions and Feelings completes the trilogy alongside Sales Logic — bringing together structure, emotion, and final decision clarity.

It builds on logic by introducing how emotion is expressed, communicated, and experienced in real-world interactions.

After mastering logical structure, this book explores how to tell your story with emotion — how to connect, influence, and create meaningful impact through what people feel.

It then ties everything together with the final piece: feelings. After logic and emotion are presented, the ultimate question becomes — how does your prospect feel about moving forward?

This final layer goes beyond surface emotion, focusing on deep internal clarity and alignment — helping ensure decisions feel right, not just sound right.

If AI is not your path, this becomes the natural next step after Sales Logic, completing the full framework.

Energy

Energy — Where does energy play its role in this sales universe? We examine the ways energy can be created, controlled, contained, and channeled in the direction we want to channel it.

The word Energy comes up often as you dive deeper into the human mind, emotions, feelings, and logic — it all ties together with Energy.

We also take some twists and unforeseen turns in this book — beyond Energy — to truly put the bow on the five core books that drive Dream Chaser University.

I hope you enjoy the thrilling conclusion of this journey.
